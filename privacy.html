Privacy Policy for GoodlyStories

Last updated: 2025-07-27

Welcome to GoodlyStories! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the App.

Information We Collect

GoodlyStories collects information to provide and improve our service to you. This includes:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and allow you to set a display name. This information is used for account management, identification within the App, and communication.
  • User-Generated Content: We collect and store the content you create and upload, such as stories (text, audio file references, titles, descriptions, genres, cover image references), comments, and series information.
  • App Preferences: We may store your preferences such as theme settings, notification preferences, and audio settings to personalize your experience.
  • Usage Data: We automatically collect information on how you interact with the App, such as features used, content viewed, and actions taken. This data helps us understand user behavior and improve app performance and functionality. This may include crash reports and performance diagnostics.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic device information (e.g., device model, operating system version) for compatibility purposes and to optimize the App for your device. This may also include device identifiers used by our third-party services for analytics or service functionality.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in the following ways:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the GoodlyStories App service.
  • To manage your account and provide customer support.
  • To personalize your experience within the App based on your preferences and interactions.
  • To improve the functionality, performance, and user experience of the App.
  • To send you important updates, security alerts, and administrative messages.
  • To ensure the security of our App, prevent fraud, and enforce our terms of service.
  • To analyze usage trends and gather demographic information for aggregate use.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is primarily stored and processed using Firebase services, which employ industry-standard security measures. We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes encryption of data in transit (e.g., via HTTPS) and at rest where provided by our service partners. However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Third-Party Services

We utilize the following third-party services to provide and improve GoodlyStories:

  • Firebase (a Google LLC service): Used for user authentication, database storage (Cloud Firestore), file storage (Cloud Storage), app performance monitoring, and potentially analytics and cloud messaging. Firebase has its own privacy policy, which you can review at https://policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Google Play Services: Used for app distribution, updates, and other core Android functionalities. Its data handling is covered under Google's Privacy Policy.

These services may collect information as described in their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services to understand how they collect, use, and share your information.

Children's Privacy

GoodlyStories is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or a higher age threshold if stipulated by local laws). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under this age. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under the specified age without parental consent, we will take steps to delete such information as soon as possible. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
